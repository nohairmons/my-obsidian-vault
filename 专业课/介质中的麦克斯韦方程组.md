| 定律名称           | 微分形式 (Differential Form)                                                             | 积分形式 (Integral Form)                                                                                                                       |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **有介质的高斯电场定律** | $$\nabla \cdot \mathbf{D} = \rho_f$$                                                 | $$\oint_{\partial V} \mathbf{D} \cdot d\mathbf{S} = Q_{f,\text{encl}}$$                                                                    |
| **高斯磁场定律**     | $$\nabla \cdot \mathbf{B} = 0$$                                                      | $$\oint_{\partial V} \mathbf{B} \cdot d\mathbf{S} = 0$$                                                                                    |
| **法拉第电磁感应定律**  | $$\nabla \times \mathbf{E} = -\frac{\partial \mathbf{B}}{\partial t}$$               | $$\oint_{\partial S} \mathbf{E} \cdot d\mathbf{l} = -\frac{d}{dt} \iint_S \mathbf{B} \cdot d\mathbf{S}$$                                   |
| **麦克斯韦-安培定律**  | $$\nabla \times \mathbf{H} = \mathbf{J}_f + \frac{\partial \mathbf{D}}{\partial t}$$ | $$\oint_{\partial S} \mathbf{H} \cdot d\mathbf{l} = I_{f,\text{encl}} + \iint_S \frac{\partial \mathbf{D}}{\partial t} \cdot d\mathbf{S}$$ |
