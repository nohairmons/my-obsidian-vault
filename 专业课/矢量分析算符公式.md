为了达到和表格一样的**对比效果**，我将内容按照**算符种类**为你重新进行了平行梳理，这样你可以非常直观地对比它们在不同坐标系下的变形：

## 1. 坐标系与拉梅系数基础

- **通用正交系 $(q_1, q_2, q_3)$ **：拉梅系数 $(h_1, h_2, h_3)$
    
- **圆柱坐标系 $(r, \phi, z)$ **：拉梅系数 $h_1=1$, $h_2=r$, $h_3=1$
    
- **球坐标系 $(r, \theta, \phi)$ **：拉梅系数 $h_1=1$, $h_2=r$, $h_3=r\sin\theta$
    

## 2. 梯度 (Gradient) $\nabla f$

- **通用形式:**
    
    $$\nabla f = \frac{1}{h_1}\frac{\partial f}{\partial q_1}\mathbf{e}_1 + \frac{1}{h_2}\frac{\partial f}{\partial q_2}\mathbf{e}_2 + \frac{1}{h_3}\frac{\partial f}{\partial q_3}\mathbf{e}_3$$
    
- **圆柱坐标:**
    
    $$\nabla f = \frac{\partial f}{\partial r}\mathbf{e}_r + \frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial f}{\partial \phi}\mathbf{e}_\phi + \frac{\partial f}{\partial z}\mathbf{e}_z$$
    
- **球坐标:**
    
    $$\nabla f = \frac{\partial f}{\partial r}\mathbf{e}_r + \frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial f}{\partial \theta}\mathbf{e}_\theta + \frac{1}{r\sin\theta}\frac{\partial f}{\partial \phi}\mathbf{e}_\phi$$
    

## 3. 散度 (Divergence) $\nabla \cdot \mathbf{A}$

- **通用形式:**
    
    $$\nabla \cdot \mathbf{A} = \frac{1}{h_1 h_2 h_3} \left[ \frac{\partial}{\partial q_1}(A_1 h_2 h_3) + \frac{\partial}{\partial q_2}(A_2 h_1 h_3) + \frac{\partial}{\partial q_3}(A_3 h_1 h_2) \right]$$
    
- **圆柱坐标:**
    
    $$\nabla \cdot \mathbf{A} = \frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial}{\partial r}(r A_r) + \frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial A_\phi}{\partial \phi} + \frac{\partial A_z}{\partial z}$$
    
- **球坐标:**
    
    $$\nabla \cdot \mathbf{A} = \frac{1}{r^2}\frac{\partial}{\partial r}(r^2 A_r) + \frac{1}{r\sin\theta}\frac{\partial}{\partial \theta}(A_\theta \sin\theta) + \frac{1}{r\sin\theta}\frac{\partial A_\phi}{\partial \phi}$$
    

## 4. 旋度 (Curl) $\nabla \times \mathbf{A}$

- **通用形式:**
    
    $$\nabla \times \mathbf{A} = \frac{1}{h_1 h_2 h_3} \begin{vmatrix} h_1\mathbf{e}_1 & h_2\mathbf{e}_2 & h_3\mathbf{e}_3 \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial q_1} & \frac{\partial}{\partial q_2} & \frac{\partial}{\partial q_3} \\ h_1 A_1 & h_2 A_2 & h_3 A_3 \end{vmatrix}$$
    
- **圆柱坐标:**
    
    $$\nabla \times \mathbf{A} = \left(\frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial A_z}{\partial \phi} - \frac{\partial A_\phi}{\partial z}\right)\mathbf{e}_r + \left(\frac{\partial A_r}{\partial z} - \frac{\partial A_z}{\partial r}\right)\mathbf{e}_\phi + \frac{1}{r}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial r}(r A_\phi) - \frac{\partial A_r}{\partial \phi}\right)\mathbf{e}_z$$
    
- **球坐标:**
    
    $$\nabla \times \mathbf{A} = \frac{1}{r\sin\theta}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial \theta}(A_\phi \sin\theta) - \frac{\partial A_\theta}{\partial \phi}\right)\mathbf{e}_r + \frac{1}{r}\left(\frac{1}{\sin\theta}\frac{\partial A_r}{\partial \phi} - \frac{\partial}{\partial r}(r A_\phi)\right)\mathbf{e}_\theta + \frac{1}{r}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial r}(r A_\theta) - \frac{\partial A_r}{\partial \theta}\right)\mathbf{e}_\phi$$
    

## 5. 拉普拉斯算子 (Laplacian) $\nabla^2 f$

- **通用形式:**
    
    $$\nabla^2 f = \frac{1}{h_1 h_2 h_3} \left[ \frac{\partial}{\partial q_1}\left(\frac{h_2 h_3}{h_1}\frac{\partial f}{\partial q_1}\right) + \frac{\partial}{\partial q_2}\left(\frac{h_1 h_3}{h_2}\frac{\partial f}{\partial q_2}\right) + \frac{\partial}{\partial q_3}\left(\frac{h_1 h_2}{h_3}\frac{\partial f}{\partial q_3}\right) \right]$$
    
- **圆柱坐标:**
    
    $$\nabla^2 f = \frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial}{\partial r}\left(r\frac{\partial f}{\partial r}\right) + \frac{1}{r^2}\frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial \phi^2} + \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial z^2}$$
    
- **球坐标:**
    
    $$\nabla^2 f = \frac{1}{r^2}\frac{\partial}{\partial r}\left(r^2\frac{\partial f}{\partial r}\right) + \frac{1}{r^2\sin\theta}\frac{\partial}{\partial \theta}\left(\sin\theta\frac{\partial f}{\partial \theta}\right) + \frac{1}{r^2\sin^2\theta}\frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial \phi^2}$$
